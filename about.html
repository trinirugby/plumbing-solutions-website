Our Story

Company History

Maintenance is at the core of what we do: our department handles roughly 160–190 jobs a month, with a 24/7 on-call team, radio- and GPS-equipped trucks, and a fully computerized dispatch and invoicing system.

  1. 1992

    G.C.I. Sprinkler Systems founded

    Began installing irrigation systems across Trinidad & Tobago.

  2. 1994

    First commercial job

    Coco Reef Resort & Spa, Tobago — the first of 65+ irrigation systems installed under G.C.I.

  3. 1996

    Diversification into plumbing

    G.C.I. diversifies, laying the groundwork for a full-service plumbing division.

  4. 1997

    Plumbing Solutions established

    Grew from one vehicle and one plumber to a 65+ staff operation serving residential and commercial clients region-wide.

  5. 2012

    World Plumbing Day, Trinidad & Tobago

    Organized T&T's World Plumbing Day launch on March 9, 2012.
